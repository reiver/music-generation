Histor of Music Generation

All histories are wrong, but some are useful.

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

History is always incomplete, very biased, missing important information, and very often misleading. However, it can still be useful to look at the past.

Era Description Culture(s) Notes
c. 2600–1800 BCE Ancient Egyptian, Old & Middle Kingdoms

The historical record is limited, but there seemed to have been tuning systems, ordered pitch progression, standardized musical expectations.

c. 1800–1500 BCE Mesopotamian Tuning Tablet Ancient Mesopotamia

An ancient Babylonian clay tablet inscribed in cuneiform that records aspects of musical tuning and theory.
